About Me

My name is Shivani Panchmatia. I’m someone who believes life is best lived with curiosity and creativity. When I’m not exploring the world of coding (and occasionally arguing with my laptop about why my code won’t run), you’ll probably find me lost in a good book. I’m deeply passionate about storytelling in all its forms — whether it’s creating podcasts, writing poetry, or publishing my thoughts for the world to read. I’ve self-published three books on Amazon, which means I’ve officially experienced the joys of being both the writer and the marketing team. I enjoy turning ideas into words, words into stories, and stories into something that (hopefully!) makes people think, smile, or feel understood.

Work Experience

I’m known for my calm presence, sharp instincts, and exceptional listening skills—blending professionalism with empathy, and just enough humor to keep things human.
